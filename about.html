About SuntriSolar

The company was brought to life by an enthusiastic individuals with a keen interest in solar energy.

This journey was sparked from their time in the construction industry, where they identified the untapped potential of solar energy. Motivated by their passion and expertise, they decided to establish a business aiming to foster a more sustainable future.

MISSION

Energizing Communities,
Empowering Change

  • To empower communities in Batangas and beyond through the use of solar energy.
  • To provide innovative, sustainable, and affordable solar solutions for homes and businesses.
  • To protect the environment while building a brighter and cleaner future for all.

VISION

Illuminating a Sustainable
Tomorrow

  • To lead the growth of renewable energy in the Philippines, especially in Batangas.
  • To enable every home and community to harness the power of the sun.
  • To empower lives, protect the planet, and inspire a brighter future for generations.
